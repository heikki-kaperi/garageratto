Versatile maintenance for cars and mopeds

I offer reliable maintenance and repair services for cars and mopeds. I also have a service vehicle, which I use to pick up mopeds for maintenance or to conveniently perform smaller jobs on site, including revitalizing old, stalled cars. Flexible help for many problems.

Services include all basic maintenance services

For cars / vans - Brake maintenance
- Oil/fluid maintenance
- Chassis maintenance
- Bodywork (welding/repair)
- Basic interior cleaning
- Tire work
- Engine maintenance

For motorcycles / mopeds - Chain maintenance
- Front sprocket replacement
- Engine repairs
- Equipment / modification
- Condition checks, also on site

 Also special services such as equipment / modifications of mopeds and motorcycles, as well as maintenance.

In the event of a problem on the road, also on-site diagnosis and maintenance assistance. Enthusiast-friendly, very flexible services. Please contact me by message or phone.
